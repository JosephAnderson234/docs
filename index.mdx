## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Obtain your access token">
    Call `POST /auth/login` with your email and password. The response returns an `accessToken` (valid for 15 minutes) and sets an `httpOnly` `refreshToken` cookie.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate your requests">
    Include the bearer token on every protected endpoint:
    ```http
    Authorization: Bearer <accessToken>
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh when the token expires">
    When the access token expires, call `POST /auth/refresh`. Your browser or HTTP client automatically sends the cookie — no body needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your workflow">
    Create an organization, add a contact, open a lead, log activities, and close with a quotation. Follow the [Core Concepts](/concepts/leads) guides for the full flow.
  </Step>
</Steps>
